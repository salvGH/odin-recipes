RAMEN

Decription

Ramen is a warm and comforting soup packed with umami. The soup contains tender noodles, flavored broth, sliced pork, soft boiled eggs, scallions and nori.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of chicken or pork broth
  • 2 packs of dried ramen noodles
  • 6 oz. of sliced pork belly
  • 2 soft boiled eggs
  • 2 sheets of nori
  • 2 scallions
  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1.2 tsp of ginger

Steps

  1. Cook noodles until desired firmness
  2. Heat broth in a pot large enough for all Ingredients, adding soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic and ginger
  3. Prepare sliced pork, scallions, and soft boiled eggs, cutting to desired sizes
  4. once broth is brought to a slow boil, add noodles in bowl, pour broth over top of them, add toppings
