Ramsay Eggs

Description

Gordan Ramsay's scrambled eggs are rich and creamy with a soft, custard like texture. Cooked low and slow with constant stirring, theyre infused with butter and finished with creme fraiche.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp creme fraiche
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a cold saucepan
  2. Add butter and place the pan over medium-low heat
  3. Stir constantly with a spatula, moving eggs off heat every 30 seconds to prevent overcooking
  4. Once creamy and slightly thickened, remove from heat and stir in creme fraiche
  5. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with chives
