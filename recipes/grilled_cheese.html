Grilled Cheese

Description

A classic comfort food. Made with a crispy golden brown bread and melted cheese toasted until perfection. A timeless favorite

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • cheese of your choice
  • mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Spread mayo on each slice of bread
  2. Heat pan over medium-low heat
  3. Place one slice of bread in pan and layer with cheese
  4. top with second slice of bread, cooking until golden brown, flipping bread every 2 minutes
  5. Remove from heat and enjoy
